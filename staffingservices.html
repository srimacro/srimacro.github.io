Strategic Staffing Solutions

We offer a wide range of IT staffing services, including contract, contract-to-hire and direct hire solutions. Our staffing solutions are tailored to suit strategic need of your business. We have a great team of recruiters who are experts in identifying qualified people for our clients. We provide suitable training to our recruiters on technical screening strategies, and candidate sourcing methods to consistently add value to our clients.

Contract/Temporary Technical Staffing

Veralgo is the right partner to meet your staffing needs for contingent and temporary workforce. We specialize in providing qualified technical resources to our clients in a very short time span. We constantly work with our clients to predict demand and develop staffing strategies. Veralgo assists clients by supplying individual consultants or entire projects teams to meet project deadlines.

Contract to Hire

Contract-to-hire is an optimal solution for clients who want to determine if a candidate is a great fit in terms of technical experience and culture. In this scenario, the candidate will be on Veralgo’s payroll and receives good benefits package for an agreed upon period time prior to becoming an employee at our clients’ organization.

Direct Hire Placement

We offer exclusive Dire Hire solutions to recruit most qualified IT professional, which can save your valuable time and money. We have developed an innovative recruiting methodology to match individuals’ skills with your job recruitment. Our recruiter also gives importance to your corporate culture and team dynamics along with required technical skills during candidate evaluation process. We strive to align great companies with great people.

If you are seeking skilled, experienced professionals for your short-term, long-term or permanent business needs, Veralgo has a solution for you
